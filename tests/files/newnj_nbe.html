Board Meetings

Notice and Schedule of Board Meetings

In accordance with the Open Public Meetings Act, the date, time, and location of all scheduled meetings of the Newark Board of Education is listed below. In the event of a change in the schedule, notification will be provided on this page and by email to all parties who have requested such notification. If Newark Public Schools are closed or dismissed early (for example, due to inclement weather), any Board meeting scheduled on that day will be cancelled. If Newark Public Schools have a delayed opening, any Board meeting scheduled on that day will remain scheduled.

All Board meetings are open to the public except for closed sessions as permitted by law. The schedule below shows the location of each meeting. During the period of declared public emergency (i.e. COVID related), all meetings will be conducted remotely. At other times, monthly Business meetings are typically held at the District’s Central Office, 765 Broad Street, Newark, NJ.

Attending Board Meetings

Requests for notice of schedule of Board meetings and any change in schedule may be submitted to npsboardrelations@nullnps.k12.nj.us

On the day of each meeting and thereafter, the meeting agenda is available on the District’s website at Electronic School Board System by clicking on the date of the meeting.
